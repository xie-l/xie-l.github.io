Interpersonal relationships

Interpersonal relationships refer to the relations and interactions between people. They are integral to individual and social functioning as humans are fundamentally social creatures. The relationships we build connect us to others in meaningful ways, meeting our needs for belonging, love, and support.

Healthy interpersonal relationships require effort and care. By building trust, maintaining communication, resolving conflicts, and showing affection and appreciation, we can enhance our existing relationships and forge strong new ones. However, relationships may also involve pain, hurt, or disappointment at some point, so relationship skills are vital for navigating challenges and sustaining healthy connections.

Exploring the dynamics of interpersonal relationships promises insights into human behavior, well-being, and what it means to share our lives with others. Our relationships shape our identities, worldviews, and pathways in life. Their complexity reflects all that relationships demand - but also the richness they provide when we lead with understanding, empathy and goodwill. Overall, relationships make life beautiful when nourished, and tragic when neglected. Their study merits our dedicated curiosity and care.

How to handle requests from others?

回到人际关系的问题，我们可以用一个新的角度来解释。

A对B提出要求，如果B无条件接受了，就释放出一个信息，那就是B有义务为A做事
长此以往，A不仅不会感激，反而觉得这理想当然。

原本是A欠B的，但是却被反向合理化了，变成B帮助A是理所当然的。不然他怎么会答应无条件帮忙?

这是个很奇怪的逻辑，但是在社会交往中，却很经常看到。

所以我们甚至可以讲，B无条件帮忙，把他自己的社交地位拉低了，成为了低价值的“工具人”。
