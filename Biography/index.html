Xie Liang is currently a third-year PhD candidate in the School of Energy Science and Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in Heilongjiang Province, China. He received his Bachelor’s degree in Energy and Power Engineering from HIT in 2016. His current research is mainly based on theoretical calculations to study synergies in energy electrocatalysis, which are prevalent in catalysts (between non-metallic heteroatoms, between non-metals and metal heteroatoms) and at interfaces (between solvents and catalysts).

  • PhD candidate in Power Engineering and Engineering Thermophysics, School of Energy Science and Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology, 2020-.
