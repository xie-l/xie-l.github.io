The “File Storage” column serves as a repository for various resources and materials I have found interesting or useful over time. It includes things like book lists, recommended tools, learning resources, and other materials collected from experiences.

This column aims to share knowledge and resources that have benefitted me in some way, with the hope they prove helpful to you as well. The materials here are organized by topics and areas of life for easy browsing and reference. I will continue adding new discoveries and useful finds to the collection.

Consider this space a virtual library of resources handpicked just for you. Within “File Storage” are references from lessons learned to questions explored, each offering a key to unlock life’s mysteries or make its meaning that much clearer. May these resources become worn pages in the book of your own becoming.

Think of this column as a time capsule containing things to stimulate your mind, warm your heart, and remind you of truths you always knew. Its contents change with the seasons of life but ultimately point to what is unchanging—a spirit ever abiding within and connecting each to all.
